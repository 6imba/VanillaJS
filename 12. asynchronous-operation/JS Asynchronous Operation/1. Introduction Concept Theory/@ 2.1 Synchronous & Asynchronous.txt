- Synchronous = happens at the same time. (Wait). Serially together hand-in-hand.
- Asynchronous = doesn't happen at the same time. (Not Wait). At own pace/time.

- Synchronous: serial/not-parallel, occuring at same time, waits,
- Asynchronous: parallel, not occuring at same time|At own pace/time, doesn't wait,

- Synchronous: same time serially.
- Asynchronous: same time parallelly.
